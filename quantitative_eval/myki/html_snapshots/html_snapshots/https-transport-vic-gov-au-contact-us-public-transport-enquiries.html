If your online feedback needs a response, we'll get back to you within 10 business days.

Phone

Call us on 1800 800 007. For TTY (teletypewriter) call 9619 2727.

Call centre hours:

  • Sunday to Thursday: 6am to midnight
  • Friday and Saturday: 6am to 6am (24 hours).


Always call Triple Zero (000) in an emergency situation.
